Who is Beau Bonbon?

Beau Bonbon is an independent sweets shop that has been open since the 1940s, now owned by the great grandson of the man who started it all. The shop has been through many changes but still has the same decorations on the inside like the original had. Always making fresh and new items every season Beau bonbon will always stay in style and will be the sweet shop you will want to look out for!
